What our members say

1984-George Orwell

Really enjoyed this book and the discussions it provoked between the members of the book club. It was all too relevant in today's world and not something I ever would have read outside of the club. 10/10

Dylan Long

The House in the Cerulean Sea-TJ Klune

If you had pitched this book to me outside of the book club, I would have never read it, but it turns out a queer love story set within a children's home full of gnomes, blobs and the antichrist (among others) is something I needed in my life!

Rachel Small
