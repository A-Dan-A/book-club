Books We've Enjoyed

As reviewed by our members

Something Wicked This Way Comes-Ray Bradbury

Ray Bradbury has a wondefully poetic writing style, which is incredibly evocative and creates a dream-like atmosphere. This is a coming-of-age story that is quite unlike anything I've ever read before and I will definitely read more Bradbury after this.

Dylan Long

The House in the Cerulean Sea-TJ Klune

If you had pitched this book to me outside of the book club, I would have never read it, but it turns out a queer love story set within a children's home full of gnomes, blobs and the antichrist (among others) is something I needed in my life!

Rachel Small
